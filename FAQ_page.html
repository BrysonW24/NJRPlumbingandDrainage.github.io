 What type of plumbing work do you do? - Services

 How long does the job take to do?

Every job is based on it's merits, with time evaluation and an explanation of the schedule, prior to commencement.

If the time is not suitable, we will reschedule a time at your convenience.

  Are you qualified?

Nathan Robson is a qaulified tradesperson as a registered plumber and gasfitter under the Australian NSW Government fair trading license. Qualified with a tradesperson certificate on water plumbing, gas fitting, gas appliances and drainage.

Water Plumbing - Fair Trading NSW (6th of August 2015) define that the qualification entails "the work of construction, alteration, extension, disconnection, removal, maintenance, repair, renewal or clearing of any pipes or fittings of any water service connecting or intending to connect, directly or indirectly with a water main."

Gas Fitting & Gas Appliances - Fair Trading NSW (6th of August 2015) define this as "working on pipe or system of pipes, and associated fittings and equipment, that is designed to convey or control gas that is downstream from the gas supply point." Additionall "any gas burning or gas using appliance that is manufactured, adapted or designed for connection to a gas installation."

Drainage -  Fair Trading NSW (6th of August 2015) define "draining is the work of sanitary drainage being the construction, alteration, extension, disconnection, removal, maintenance, repair, renewal, ventilation, flushing or cleansing of any sanitary drain connecting, or intended to connect, directly or indirectly with a sewer. This includes work on a house drain connected to an onsite wastewater management system."

 Do you charge a call out fee?

Yes, a call out fee is only charged once, no matter how many times we need to return to your job.

A call out fee covers the expenses associated with getting a professional qualified plumber to your door, administration of your job and peace of mind with after sales service.

 Can you provide an account facility?


NJR Plumbing & Drainage can provide 7, 14 and 30 day accounts to approved business clients.
