About NJR

NJR Plumbing and Drainage was founded in 2017 and is a personally run business owned and operated by Nathan Robson. The company works from the West to the East of Sydney - why go any further?

When you call NJR you can be assured you are speaking to a fully qualified, experienced plumber who can provide you with all the services you would expect from a reliable plumbing contractor.

The business specialises in residential plumbing fit-outs, maintenance, installation and a whole range more which you can find here!

On the NJR Website you will be sure to find the plumbing services that are provided, what we can do to help, and how to get in touch to find out more if you might have any questions - it would be great to hear from you!
