SERVICES

Which of your problems is the one that needs to be looked at? Enquire for any further information.

Amenity, maintenance and alterations
Any types of fixtures that need repairing – toilets, taps, showers, hot water units etc.
 

Backflow prevention
Legally needs to be maintained annually, to ensure that contaminants do not contaminate the main water supply.

Blocked drains
Can range from many difference blockages such as build-up of grease, oils and fats, sanitary items and tree roots.

Compressed air
Compressed air performance is used in things like dental and medical surgeries. Cleaning out compressed lines, fixing blockages or burst pipes, testing and adjusting pressures and servicing on compressor units.

Fire services
All fire hydrants and hose reels must be inspected at six monthly intervals and then flow tested annually.

Heat pumps
Main usages are in hot water systems to provide an efficient hot water system by providing quick delivery of instant hot water.

Hot water systems
There are a few different types including electric, solar, gas or heat pump systems. These types provide hot water to taps.

Roofing and guttering
System that provides coverage for shelter and is a very important part of every property to provide a safe and shelter environment.

Stormwater systems
Provides protection to the property from influx of water during storm season or a heavy downpour of rain.

Scheduled maintenance services
Take the stress away. We can arrange scheduled service maintenance calls and reminders to ensure your plumbing is up-to-date and compliant quality standard servicing so that you do not need to worry about anything.

Thermostatic mixing valves (TMV)
Blends hot water with cold water to ensure constant safe shower and bath temperatures to prevent scolding.

Trade waste / grease traps
All liquid waste generated on commercial premises and discharged to the main sewage system that is not human waste. If not managed, properly trade waste can damage the sewage system and the environment.

Water filtration systems
Process of removing undesirable chemicals, biological contaminants, suspended solids and gases from contaminated water.
